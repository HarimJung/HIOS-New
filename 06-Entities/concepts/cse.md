# Comprehensive Sexuality Education (CSE)

A curriculum-based process of teaching and learning about cognitive, emotional,
physical and social aspects of sexuality. Aims to equip children and young people
with knowledge, skills, attitudes and values that will empower them to realize
their health, well-being and dignity.

## Key Facts
- 130+ countries have some form of CSE policy
- UNESCO International Technical Guidance on Sexuality Education (ITGSE) is the global standard
- UNFPA is the lead UN agency for CSE implementation support
