## AI 요약 (Granola)

### New Scenario Performance (June 1–30)

- Four new scenarios generated 1,245 alerts in June
- Largest contributor: previously branded vehicle policy scenario
- Review rates vary widely across scenarios: 11% to 93%
  - Significant portion of alerts remain unreviewed, so assumption rates are preliminary
- No converted alerts recorded yet (scenarios only \~6 weeks in service)
- Results should be treated as operational indicators, not established performance metrics
- Members encouraged to continue reviewing alerts and share feedback on:
  - Alert relevance
  - Common discard reasons
  - Potential scenario refinements

### June Alert Volume Spike

- June recorded 1,780 alerts, highest monthly volume in 2026
  - May comparison: 481 alerts (June was \~4x higher)
- Breakdown: 209 policy-related, 571 claim-related
  - Policy alerts were the primary driver of the increase
- Two factors behind the growth:
  - More candidate and cooperator records being evaluated this year
  - Four new scenarios introduced in June
- Review capacity monitoring to begin as volume continues to grow

### Acceptance Working Sessions Update

- Six member working sessions held between April and June 2026
- Completed detailed review of activation logic and explanatory variables across 13 scenarios:
  - 9 claims-related, 4 policy-related
- Prior damage enhancements identified as high priority based on member feedback, incorporated into upcoming release
- Sessions continue as a key feedback mechanism for EQ Insights evolution
- Cadence going forward still TBD:
  - Likely biweekly or monthly, to be aligned with release schedule
  - Member survey planned to gauge preferred cadence (David to send email)

### Planned Enhancements This Quarter

- Scenario enhancements (logic-level changes based on working session feedback):
  - Prior damage detection improvements
  - High-interest claim scenario for elevated risk visibility
  - Removal of third-party vehicle alerting to reduce noise
  - Further scenario refinements and optimizations
- Platform/model improvements:
  - Model tuning to improve scoring accuracy and rank higher-risk claims
  - New triage step in workflow for low-qualification alerts, enabling structured review before deep investigation
- Expected outcomes: fewer false positives, higher-value alert focus, improved workflow efficiency

### Entity Resolution Feedback

- Member flagged instances of duplicate entities in the network viewer:
  - Multiple individuals, policies, and vehicles with near-identical information appearing as separate nodes
  - Causes issues when spidering off entities, especially for newer users
- Entity resolution enhancements not currently planned for this quarter
- Team to include member-provided examples in the existing ticket and prioritize accordingly

### Next Steps

- **Send member survey on working session cadence** (David)

  Query members on preferred frequency before finalizing biweekly or monthly schedule.

- **Share entity resolution examples with the team**

  Member to provide specific duplicate-entity examples for inclusion in the existing ticket.
