# DEI Impact Dashboard
### If You Can't Measure It, You Can't Improve It

---

## Slide 1: Title

**DEI Impact Dashboard**
*Turning diversity, equity & inclusion from intentions into measurable outcomes*

Harim Jung — Data Visualization & AI Automation
For: Equitee

---

## Slide 2: The Problem

**DEI programs are well-intentioned but poorly measured.**

- **76% of companies** have DEI initiatives, but only **23% can
  quantify their impact** (McKinsey, 2024)
- DEI reporting is manual: spreadsheets, surveys, annual PDF reports
- No real-time visibility into progress vs. targets
- Benchmarking against peers requires expensive consulting engagements

---

## Slide 3: Who Feels This

**HR Directors & DEI Officers**
- Tasked with "improving diversity" but lacking baseline metrics
- Reporting to C-suite with anecdotal evidence, not data

**Corporate Leadership**
- ESG reporting demands quantifiable DEI metrics
- Board diversity targets with no tracking mechanism

**DEI Consultancies**
- Need scalable tools to serve multiple clients
- Current approach: custom spreadsheets per client

## Slide 5: The Solution

**One dashboard. All DEI metrics. Multilingual.**

1. **TRACK** — Real-time DEI metrics across hiring, retention,
   promotion, pay equity, representation
2. **BENCHMARK** — Compare against industry peers and targets
3. **REPORT** — Auto-generated reports (quarterly, annual, ESG-compliant)
4. **ACT** — AI recommendations based on data patterns

---

## Slides 6-8: Features

**Metrics tracked:**
- Representation by level, department, geography
- Hiring pipeline diversity (application → offer → hire)
- Retention rates by demographic group
- Pay equity analysis (adjusted/unadjusted)
- Promotion velocity by group
- Inclusion survey scores over time

**Reporting:**
- One-click quarterly PDF/slideshow generation
- ESG/CSR compliance format
- Board-ready executive summary
- Multilingual: French, English, Korean

**Benchmarking:**
- Anonymous industry comparisons
- Year-over-year trend tracking
- Target vs. actual gap analysis

---

## Slide 9: Sample Output

**Dashboard view:**
- Top bar: overall diversity score (0-100) with trend arrow
- Grid: 4 cards (hiring, retention, promotion, pay equity)
- Each card: current metric, target, gap, trend chart
- Filter: department, geography, time period

**Auto-report excerpt:**
"Q2 2026: Representation of women in senior leadership increased
from 28% to 31%, closing 60% of the gap toward the 33% target.
Hiring pipeline diversity improved at application stage (+8%) but
conversion to offer remained flat. Recommended action: review
interview panel composition."
