## Caption
Nigeria's stock market just overtook Korea's KOSPI as the world's top performer in dollar terms (Bloomberg, July 2026).

The interesting part isn't the ranking — it's the mechanics. Cooling AI sentiment pulled money out of AI-heavy markets, and in a frontier market, even modest inflows send prices vertical. Add a stabilizing currency, and you get a #1 nobody predicted in January.

I spend my work life on data in emerging economies, and this is the pattern every time: the "best market in the world" rotates constantly, and dollar-vs-local-currency framing changes the entire story.
