# Visual Climate — Personal Brand

## Brand Identity
Visual Climate = Harim's personal brand at the intersection of
data visualization, climate/development, and AI automation.

## Platforms & Content Types
| Platform | Format | Language | Cadence |
|----------|--------|----------|---------|
| YouTube | Longform (10-20min) | Korean + EN subs | 2/month |
| YouTube | Shorts (30-60s) | Korean | 4/month |
| Xiaohongshu | Image + short text | Chinese (Simplified) | 3/week |
| LinkedIn | Articles, carousel | English | 2/week |
| Newsletter | Long-read | Korean/English | 2/month |

## Content Pillars
1. **Climate Data Visualization** — making climate data accessible and beautiful
2. **AI for International Development** — automation tools for UN/NGO work
3. **Money & Side Hustles** — monetizing data skills, consulting income
4. **Behind the Scenes** — real consulting life, tools, workflows

## Voice Guide
- Analytical yet warm. Professional but accessible.
- Korean content: natural mix of formal/informal, code-switching allowed.
- Xiaohongshu: friendly, practical Chinese. Emojis natural.
- English: professional but conversational, avoid jargon.
- Never clickbait. Data-backed claims only.

## Monetization
- YouTube AdSense + sponsorships
- Xiaohongshu brand deals
- Consulting leads (funnel from content)
- Digital products (templates, courses — future)

## Product Decks (in 05-Product-Decks/)
Each consulting area has a pitch document:
- `deck-cse-toolkit.md` — CSE Knowledge Toolkit
- `deck-climate-literacy.md` — Climate Data Literacy Engine
- `deck-dei-dashboard.md` — DEI Impact Dashboard
- `deck-ai-automation.md` — AI Automation for Development Sector

## Content Compounding
One consulting task → multiple content pieces:
- YouTube longform (process/tutorial)
- YouTube short (before/after, key insight)
- Xiaohongshu (tips in Chinese, NOT direct translation)
- LinkedIn (English professional insight)
- Newsletter (monthly "What I shipped")

## Rules for This Project
- Thumbnail concepts: bold text, face/data visual, contrast colors.
- Xiaohongshu posts must pass cultural review — no direct translation.
- All drafts go through voice-guide check before publishing.
- Track performance weekly: views, saves, comments, subscriber growth.
